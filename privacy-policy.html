Privacy Policy

We connect you to the best cleaning, babysitting, electrical, transportation professionals and much more.

Your privacy is fundamental to us. This Privacy Policy explains how MCLeanConnect collects, uses, stores and shares your personal information when using our application.

1. Information Collected

  • Registration Data: Name, email, phone and other information provided at the time of registration.
  • Profile: You can add photo, location, services offered, qualifications and other optional data.
  • App Usage: We record pages accessed, features used, searches performed and interactions.
  • Payments: Payments for services are made directly between client and service provider, outside the application.
  • Commission: For each client acquired through the app, the service provider must pay a commission fee to MCLeanConnect, according to rules and values informed on the platform.
  • Communications: We store messages and interactions between users to ensure safety and quality.
  • Location: We may collect location data to connect nearby clients and service providers and display relevant services.

2. Use of Information

  • To operate, maintain and improve the application and its services.
  • To personalize your experience and suggest services suitable to your profile and location.
  • For communication about updates, promotions, support and relevant information.
  • To process payments and ensure transaction security.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse and comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing

  • With other users: Your profile may be viewed by other users to facilitate service hiring.
  • With partners and suppliers: We share data only with companies that assist us in app operation (payments, hosting, marketing), always under confidentiality agreement.
  • With authorities: We may share information through court order or legal obligation.
  • Anonymized data may be used for analysis and service improvements.

4. User Rights

  • You can access, correct, update or delete your personal data at any time through the application or by contacting us.
  • You can opt out of receiving promotional communications.
  • You have the right to file a complaint with the data protection authority, if applicable.

5. Security

  • We adopt technical and administrative measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss or misuse.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience and analyze app usage.

7. Children

  • The application is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not intentionally collect children's data.

8. International Transfer

  • Your data may be processed in other countries, always respecting this policy and current legislation.

9. Policy Changes

  • We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you about relevant changes through the application or email.

10. Contact

  • If you have any questions, contact us by email: mcleanconnet@gmail.com.
